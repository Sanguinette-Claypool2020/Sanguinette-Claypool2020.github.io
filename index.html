Their Love Story

  • How They Met

    December 10, 2017

    Tyler and Abby met on Facebook. After exchanging a messages over several days, they decided to finally meet.

  • The First Date

    December 15, 2017

    They went ice skating on the coldest night at Steinberg Skating Rink. Afterwards they ate Texas Roadhouse for dinner.
